This simulation demonstrates the classic Lotka-Volterra predator-prey model:

  • Blue line: Prey (rabbits) population
  • Red line: Predator (foxes) population

The oscillations show how predator and prey populations are linked: more prey leads to more predators, which then reduces prey, which reduces predators, and the cycle repeats.
